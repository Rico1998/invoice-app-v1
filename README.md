# Invoice App - Zizo Capital

This is a local invoice generation tool built for Zizo Capital.

## How to Use
1.  **Open the App**: Double-click the `index.html` file in this folder. It will open in your web browser.
2.  **Dashboard**: This is your home screen showing your invoice history and total revenue.
3.  **Create Invoice**: Click "New Invoice" to start. 
    - The **Due Date** automatically sets to 30 days from today (Net 30).
    - Data is saved automatically to your browser.
4.  **Print/PDF**: To save an invoice as a PDF, click "View" -> "Print / Save PDF".
5.  **Email**: Click "Email Client" to open a draft in your email application.

## Important Note
- **Data Storage**: All your invoices are saved in your **browser's local storage** on this specific computer. 
- **Backups**: If you clear your browser history/cache, you might lose your saved invoices. It is recommended to download a PDF copy of every invoice you create.
